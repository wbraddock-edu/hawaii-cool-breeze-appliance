FAQ

Common questions about appliance repair in Hawaii.

Do you offer same-day appliance repair on Hawaii Island? +
Yes! For most service calls booked by noon, we can dispatch a technician the same day. For afternoon bookings, we typically schedule for the following morning. We understand that a broken refrigerator or washer can't wait days for repair, so same-day service is our priority. Call (808) 555-0123 for urgent same-day requests.
What brands do you repair? +
We service all major appliance brands including LG, Samsung, Whirlpool, GE, Maytag, KitchenAid, Frigidaire, Bosch, Kenmore, Sub-Zero, Viking, Thermador, Miele, Electrolux, and Amana. Our technicians receive ongoing brand-specific training to stay current with the latest models and diagnostic technologies.
How much does appliance repair cost in Hawaii? +
Our service call fee is $79 for most appliances ($89 for AC units), which includes diagnosis. Parts and labor for the actual repair are quoted before we start work. We never begin a repair without your approval of the estimate. Common repairs range from $150-$400 total. We believe in transparent, upfront pricing.
Do you provide a warranty on repairs? +
Yes. All parts replaced carry a 90-day warranty. If the same component fails within 90 days of repair, we replace it at no charge for parts or labor. This warranty applies to parts only — if a different component fails, that's a separate service call.
Do you service all of Hawaii Island or just Hilo? +
We cover all of Hawaii Island, including Hilo, Keaau, Pahoa, Kona, Waimea, Waikoloa, Pahala, and surrounding areas. Travel fees may apply for locations more than 40 miles from our Hilo base. Call or book online to confirm service availability for your area.
Should I repair or replace my appliance? +
It depends on the appliance age, repair cost, and replacement cost. We'll always give you an honest assessment. In Hawaii, replacement costs are higher than on the mainland due to shipping, making repair more often the better financial choice. If repair cost exceeds 60-70% of the cost to replace, we'll typically tell you that replacement may be worth considering.
Can you repair appliances that are still under manufacturer warranty? +
If your appliance is under manufacturer warranty, contact the manufacturer first — they may cover the repair at no cost. If they won't dispatch a technician quickly enough, we can perform the repair, though warranty reimbursement would then be your responsibility. We can provide documentation for warranty claims.
What should I do while waiting for my repair appointment? +
For refrigerators: move perishables to a cooler or neighbor's fridge if possible. For washers: use a laundromat for essential loads. For ovens: use microwave, air fryer, or eat out. For AC: use fans and close blinds during hottest hours. Most importantly, don't attempt DIY repairs on appliances — incorrect disassembly can make the repair more complex and expensive.
Do you carry parts in your truck? +
Yes. Our service vehicles are stocked with the most commonly needed parts for all major brands we service. This allows us to complete many repairs in a single visit rather than having to order parts and return. For less common parts, we typically can source them within 1-2 business days from our Hilo parts supplier.
