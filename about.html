About Cool Breeze Appliance

Hawaii Island's trusted appliance repair specialists since 2009.

Our Story

Born in Hilo, Built for Hawaii

Cool Breeze Appliance Repair was founded in 2009 by Derek Makoa, a Hilo native who trained as an appliance technician on the mainland and came home to find that Hawaii Island lacked reliable, knowledgeable appliance repair options. The nearest major service centers were in Honolulu, leaving Big Island residents waiting days or weeks for repairs that should take hours.

The name "Cool Breeze" captures both the relief of a working AC unit on a hot Hilo afternoon and the trade winds that make Hawaii living so unique. Today, Cool Breeze is a team of 5 factory-trained technicians covering the entire Hawaii Island from Hilo to Kona, maintaining a 97% same-day service rate that we're proud of.
